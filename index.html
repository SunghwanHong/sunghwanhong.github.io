About
I did my Ph.D. at Korea University in the Computer Vision Lab, supervised by Prof. Seungryong Kim. My PhD was focused on Computer Vision and its applications, especially in the tasks of image matching, 3D reconstruction, pose estimation and novel-view rendering. During my PhD I had the opportunity to intern in Microsft Research Asia, working with Chong Luo and Jiaolong Yang. I am one of the Google Ph.D. fellowship recipient of 2023. I worked as a postdoctoral researcher with Seungryong Kim at KAIST. Now I'm at ETH Zurich as a postdoctoral fellow, working with Marc Pollefeys and Konrad Shindler.

I am always open to research collaboration. Also, if you are interested in semester projects (ETHZ) or master's theses (ETHZ) related to 2D/3D computer vision, feel free to contact me via email.
